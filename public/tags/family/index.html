Getting my mother on a password manager

A few weeks back, my mother (70-odd years old) was victim of a phishing attack. Smishing to be exact. She got a text from what appeared to be the government, asking her to login to her account using her bank’s card reader. For anyone not familiar with the Belgian government’s authentication process, this is not uncommon. Turns out though that,on this occasion, this was somehow linked to a payment authorisation protocol and… well, she got scammed off a few thousand euros (!...
